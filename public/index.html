Privacy-first

Double-blind by design

Traditional networking exposes you. Event Medium protects you. Your identity stays hidden until both sides express interest — eliminating the awkwardness, politics, and cold-approach anxiety of conventional events.

Your canister is completely private — there is no public directory. All matching is anonymous and agentic. Identities are only revealed when both parties independently consent.
